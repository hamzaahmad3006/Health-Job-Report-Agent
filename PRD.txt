# 📄 Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

**Health Job Assistant (Medical Voice-to-Report System)**

---

## 1. Background & Problem Statement

Medical professionals (doctors, nurses, and caregivers) often spend significant time documenting patient encounters after consultations. Manual documentation lead to:

* Physician burnout due to excessive paperwork
* Delayed or incomplete patient charts
* Potential for missing critical medical details
* Inconsistent reporting formats across departments

Managers and administrators require **clear, structured, and clinically accurate reports** for billing, compliance, and continuity of care.

---

## 2. Product Vision

Enable healthcare professionals to **record a short patient summary** via a mobile-friendly web app and automatically convert it into a **structured medical report** using AI.

---

## 3. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals

* Reduce documentation time for medical professionals
* Convert medical voice notes into structured reports (SOAP-lite format)
* Ensure clinical consistency and data structure

### Success Metrics

* ≥ 95% medical terminology transcription accuracy
* ≤ 5 seconds end-to-end processing time
* 100% valid clinical JSON responses

---

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

* Doctors, Nurses, and Medical Assistants

### Secondary Users

* Hospital Administrators and Billing Specialists

---

## 5. User Story

> *“I am a busy doctor seeing 30 patients a day. I don't have time to type long notes. I need a secure, mobile-friendly interface where I can speak naturally about a patient encounter, and the system automatically extracts symptoms, diagnosis, and plans into a structured format.”*

---

## 6. In-Scope Features

### 6.1 Audio Ingestion (Frontend)

* Record clinical notes directly from a mobile browser
* Upload audio files
* Supported formats: `.wav`, `.mp3`, `.m4a`

### 6.2 Speech-to-Text (STT)

* Transcription handled by **Deepgram API**
* Supports medical terminology and various accents
* Smart formatting and clinical punctuation

### 6.3 LLM-Based Clinical Extraction

* Backend uses **Groq-hosted LLaMA models**
* Converts raw transcript into structured clinical data
* Identifies symptoms, potential diagnoses, and treatment plans

### 6.4 Structured Medical Report Output

The system must extract and return the following fields:

* **Patient Name**
* **Symptoms** (array)
* **Diagnosis/Assessment**
* **Treatment Plan**
* **Urgency/Sentiment** (Inferred)

---

## 7. Out of Scope (Phase 1)

* Full HIPAA-compliant storage (Phase 1 focus is MVP)
* Integration with major EMRs (Epic/Cerner)
* Prescription management

---

## 8. Functional Requirements

### FR-1: Audio Submission

Allow clinicians to record/upload audio notes.

### FR-2: Transcription

Transcribe clinical audio using Deepgram.

### FR-4: Structured Extraction

Extract medical entities using Groq-powered LLM.

### FR-5: Clinical Response

Return a validated medical JSON report.

---

## 10. System Architecture

```
Frontend (Next.js – Health UI)
  └── Voice Recording
        |
        v
Backend (Python – FastAPI)
  ├── Deepgram STT (Medical Speech → Text)
  ├── Groq LLM (Clinical Text → Structured JSON)
  ├── Clinical Schema Validation
  └── API Response
        |
        v
Structured Medical Report (JSON)
```

---

## 11. Technology Stack

### Backend

* **Python (FastAPI)**

### AI Services

* **Speech-to-Text:** Deepgram
